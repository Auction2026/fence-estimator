- Setting Posts: 2.5 hours
- Rails: 1.5 hours
- Fence Installation: 2.5 hours
- 1 Gate: 1 hour
- Finishing: 1 hour
- **Subtotal: 14 hours**

**Multipliers Applied:**
- Terrain (slight slopes): 1.25x = 17.5 hours
- Weather (spring normal): 1.0x
- Experience (intermediate crew): 0.9x = 15.75 hours
- Soil type (normal): 1.0x
- **FINAL: 15.75 hours**

**Labour Rate Calculation:**
- Base Ontario minimum: $16.55/hour
- Journeyman premium: +$10/hour = $26.55
- WCB (Fencing class 2%): +$0.53
- Benefits (vacation, stat holidays): +$4/hour = $30.55/hour
- Overhead (25%): +$7.64 = $38.19/hour
- Profit margin (35%): +$13.37 = **$51.56/hour**

**Total Labour Cost:**
- 15.75 hours × $51.56/hour = **$811.56**
